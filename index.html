About

30 years of Apple media engineering

I'm Ted Jucevic. I spent 30 years at Apple — 17 of those leading the Developer Technical Support team for Graphics & Media, the team that helped every major streaming app, camera app, and media player ship on Apple platforms.


My team was the last line of support for developers working with AVFoundation, HLS, FairPlay Streaming, Core Video, Metal, and Camera. If a Fortune 500 media company had a pipeline problem Apple's documentation couldn't solve, it came to us.


Now I bring that depth directly to companies building real-time video products — telehealth, live commerce, sports, broadcast, security, drones — anywhere sub-second latency matters and Apple platforms are the target.
